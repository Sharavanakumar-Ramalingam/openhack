Project Idea & Solution

B2C companies (e-commerce, banking, telecom, etc.) handle millions of customer complaints daily, leading to delays, high operational costs, and inconsistent resolutions. Our AI-driven chatbot automates complaint handling, reduces response time, and improves customer satisfaction.

  • AI chatbot analyzes complaints, provides instant resolutions, detects sentiment, and escalates critical issues.
  • Reduces operational costs and enhances customer experience with proactive support.
  • Automates handling of refunds, billing disputes, and delivery issues.
  • Detects frustration/urgency and adjusts responses empathetically.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: React.js - Responsive UI/UX
  • Backend: Flask (Python) - API & Server logic
  • AI: LLama, TextBlob - Sentiment analysis & chatbot intelligence
  • Database: MongoDB, Firebase - Session storage & data management

Use Cases

  • E-Commerce: Refund & Order Issues (Amazon, Flipkart, etc.)
  • Banking & Finance: Fraudulent Transactions & Dispute Resolution
  • Telecom: Billing Disputes & Plan Upgrades
  • Customer Service: Multi-Channel AI Support
  • Travel & Hospitality: Booking Issues & Cancellations
